Net Reverse Insight

Insight is a proprietary AI-powered tool for deep competitor ad analysis. Users enter up to three competitors, and Insight collects their ads from Facebook and Instagram via Meta Ads Library. It then produces a structured AI analysis covering:

  • Campaign strategy and messaging logic
  • Creative structure and visual patterns
  • Copy analysis and testing frequency
  • Strengths, weaknesses, and actionable recommendations

Insight is designed for marketers, agencies, and business owners who want to understand competitor advertising without hours of manual research.
